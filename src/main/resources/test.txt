**Ingredients:**

1. Large shrimp - 500 grams
2. Glass noodles (vermicelli) - 100 grams (soaked until soft)
3. Minced garlic - 1 tablespoon
4. Minced ginger - 1 tablespoon
5. Oyster sauce - 2 tablespoons
6. Light soy sauce - 1 tablespoon
7. Sugar - 1 teaspoon
8. Ground black pepper - 1/2 teaspoon
9. Sesame oil - 1 teaspoon
10. Chicken stock - 1/2 cup
11. Celery stalks, cut into pieces - 2-3 stalks
12. Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons

**Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the Shrimp:** Peel and devein the shrimp, leaving the tails intact. Clean thoroughly and set aside.

2. **Mix the Glass Noodles:** Combine the soaked glass noodles with the oyster sauce, light soy sauce, sugar, ground black pepper, and sesame oil. Mix well.

3. **Prepare the Pan:** Heat the vegetable oil in a pan or clay pot over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and ginger and stir-fry until fragrant.

4. **Add the Noodles:** Add the seasoned glass noodles to the pan and stir-fry until well mixed.

5. **Arrange the Shrimp:** Place the shrimp on top of the glass noodles, arranging them neatly.

6. **Add the Chicken Stock:** Pour the chicken stock into the pan, cover, and simmer over low heat for about 10-15 minutes, or until the shrimp are cooked and the noodles have absorbed the stock.

7. **Add the Celery:** Mix in the celery pieces with the noodles and shrimp.

8. **Serve:** Transfer the shrimp and glass noodles to a serving plate and serve immediately.

Enjoy your delicious shrimp with glass noodles!
